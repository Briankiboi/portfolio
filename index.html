About Me

I am a Flutter & Frontend Engineer and Full-stack Developer specializing in building cross-platform mobile apps and scalable web systems. I deliver clean, efficient code and user-friendly solutions for startups, SMEs, and established institutions.

I have worked with organizations such as LogixFleet, DMsolutions, Tharaka University, and the Judiciary - Office of the Chief Justice (Kenya), where I gained hands-on experience in software development, QA automation, mobile app development, UI/UX design, data analysis, and workflow optimization. These roles helped me strengthen my technical skills, problem-solving ability, and communication in both formal and remote work environments.

I have also worked on freelance web and software projects, where I developed and deployed websites and web applications, delivered custom features based on business requirements, provided technical consultation, and optimized systems for performance and scalability.

In addition, I am building Startup Job Readiness AI, a project focused on helping users prepare for the job market through smart, practical, and accessible technology solutions.

My technical stack includes Flutter for Android and iOS app development, React, PHP, Laravel, Go for web interfaces, Django/Python for backend systems, and HTML, CSS3, JavaScript, TypeScript, PHP, Laravel, Go, and SQL for web development. I also handle REST API integration, state management, UI/UX design, and performance optimization.

I offer clean, reliable code, complete project management, and clear communication with consistent updates. I am also certified in Project Management through Microsoft, and I hold a certification in Generative AI.
